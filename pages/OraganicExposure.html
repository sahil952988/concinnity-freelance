Why does search engine
Optimization matter?

As a seasoned SEO agency in Hong Kong, we are often asked the same question: why does SEO matter?

To answer this question, we must go back to why we need to optimize our websites for the search engines. SEO helps a website get a good rank at the search engine results pages (SERPs). When the best SEO practices are applied, your website will have a greater chance of appearing on the SERPs whenever a potential client enters a targeted keyword on a search engine’s search bar.
